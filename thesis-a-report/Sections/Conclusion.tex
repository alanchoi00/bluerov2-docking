\section{Conclusion}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Summary: Proposed a vision-based, turbidity-adaptive pose estimation system for autonomous ROV docking to a free-floating station
    \item Importance: Addresses operational limitations of current ROV deployments and enables extended mission capability
    \item Expected contributions: practical algorithms for turbidity adaptation, an integrated detection-to-control pipeline, and experimental validation on ROV hardware
    \item Limitations and scope: optical methods limited by extreme turbidity; planned fallbacks include active beacons and acoustic aids
    \item Transition to Thesis B: focus on implementation, tank validation, performance characterisation, and refinement of control strategies
\end{itemize}
